About Our Hotel

Eastern Premier Hotel is a peaceful retreat in Koforidua, offering exceptional hospitality and comfort to travelers. Established in 2010, we have become a landmark in the Eastern Region of Ghana.

Our hotel combines modern amenities with traditional Ghanaian warmth. Located just minutes from Koforidua's city center, we offer easy access to business districts, cultural attractions, and natural wonders.

With our dedicated staff, comfortable accommodations, and excellent dining options, we ensure every guest experiences the true meaning of "Akwaaba" (welcome).
